Episode 6 features Antonio Juanilla (Specter) a DevSecOps leader.

In this technical deep-dive, we explore the reality of securing modern cloud-native platforms. We move beyond the buzzwords to discuss how to actually bake security into the backend development lifecycle without breaking the developer experience. From his OSDO framework to the high-stakes world of Kubernetes security in regulated environments, Antonio shares the pragmatic approach to staying safe in 2026—all while sharing a virtual mate between Spain and the UAE.

**Topics we cover:** <br/>
- **Security Leadership:** Transitioning from hands-on engineering to directing platform security in a global tech hub.
- **The OSDO Project:**.
- **CI/CD Pipelines:** SAST and secret management and more.
- **Kubernetes in Deployment:** Best practices.
- **K8s Security:** The top misconfigurations that lead to breaches and how to prevent them.
- **Community & Events:** A look inside the upcoming X-Ops Conference and HackBarcelona.
